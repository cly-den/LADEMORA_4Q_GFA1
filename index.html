Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS)

Many stray dogs and cats are treated badly in an everyday basis. However, they aren't the only ones experiencing cruelty in our world. Animals kept in waterparks or zoos such as orcas and elephants also experience such cruelty from humans. Philippine Animal Welfare Society (PAWS) is an organization that devotes itself to protecting animals from the cruelty that humans put them through. They are able to do this through education, animal sheltering, and advocacy. I support this organization because I believe that animals lives matter too. They can keep us company and make us happy. Human actions shouldn't be the cause of their suffering. Being able to give them a chance in living a good life is a goal that I would love to achieve.
